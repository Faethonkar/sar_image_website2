Project Showcase: AI Algorithms for Vessel and Aircraft Detection

Objective

The goal of this project is to develop advanced AI algorithms for the accurate detection and classification of vessels and aircraft from Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) imagery. These algorithms aim to improve maritime surveillance, enhance situational awareness, and provide reliable data for defense and commercial applications.

Visuals

Rotating carousel showcasing SAR imagery used for aircraft detection.

Methodology

The project follows a multi-step approach involving:

  • Data Collection: Gathering SAR imagery from various sources, including satellite and airborne sensors.
  • Data Preprocessing: Enhancing SAR images by removing noise and improving resolution for better analysis.
  • AI Model Development: Using deep learning techniques, such as Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs), to train models on annotated data for vessel and aircraft detection.
  • Testing & Optimization: Validating model performance and optimizing for real-time detection across various conditions.

Results

The developed AI models demonstrated high accuracy in detecting vessels and aircraft from SAR imagery. Key results include:

  • Detection of distinct vessel types in maritime environments.
  • Successful detection of aircraft, even in challenging weather conditions or low visibility.
  • Near Real-time processing capabilities, low detection time.

Case Study

In a recent case study focused on maritime surveillance, the AI model demonstrated exceptional performance by detecting vessels in restricted waters. It achieved a detection accuracy exceeding 90%, greatly improving situational awareness and operational efficiency for naval missions.
